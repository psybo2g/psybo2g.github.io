Hi, it's Psybo2g!

Thanks for dropping by! I’m a part-time indie web and game developer, and a full-time student who’s been tinkering with code since my early teens.

For me, building games and web projects has always been less of a chore and more of a playground—I love exploring new tools and bringing ideas to life, even if I’m doing it all from an old mobile phone (no computer yet, student life!). It’s a fun constraint that pushes me to stay creative and keep things lightweight.

I mostly work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I’ve dabbled in Lua (Löve2D) and Java too. When it comes to game jams and quick prototypes, I often reach for Phaser.js—it’s just so friendly and full of handy tools.
